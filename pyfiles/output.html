Happy holidays from our
family to yours!
Holiday newsletters are a great way to keep the people closest to
you updated on your family's life. It's also a fun way to share holiday
greetings or write about your past year's experiences. To start, let
each of your family write down their own articles in the newsletter.
Add photographs from family vacations. Think about what you
would tell your family and friends if you could see them.
A family newsletter is also a fun activity to do as a family. Everyone
feels included when you share memories with each other. Sit down
and talk about the important things that happened in the past year.
Also, think about who's reading your newsletter. What would your
friends or distant relatives most want to hear about? Write down
your ideas and then organize your list into categories.
